Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data and respect your privacy while providing free HLS video streaming services.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Last Updated: January 2025

Privacy Summary

M3U8 Player Online is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We collect minimal information, do not store personal data or video content, and all streaming happens directly in your browser. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

Information We Collect

M3U8 Player Online is designed with privacy as a core principle. We are committed to collecting only the minimal information necessary to provide our service effectively:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, process, or have access to any personally identifiable information (PII) including names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses
  • No Video Content Storage: All video streaming occurs directly between your browser and the video source. We never access, intercept, store, or process your video content or M3U8 URLs
  • Anonymous Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve our service, including:
    • Page views and session duration
    • Browser type and version (for compatibility purposes)
    • General geographic location (country/region level only)
    • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet) for responsive design optimization
  • Local Browser Storage: We use browser local storage exclusively to save your preferences:
    • Theme preferences (light/dark mode)
    • Language selection
    • Player settings and configurations

How We Use Information

The limited, anonymous information we collect is used solely for the following legitimate purposes:

  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance player performance, fix bugs, and optimize user experience
  • Technical Optimization: Understanding browser compatibility and device usage to ensure our player works across all platforms
  • Infrastructure Maintenance: Monitoring service performance and maintaining reliable streaming capabilities
  • User Experience Enhancement: Remembering your preferences (theme, language) for future visits to provide a personalized experience
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing abuse, spam, or malicious activities that could harm our service or users

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain a strict no-sharing policy regarding user information. Since we don't collect personal data, there is fundamentally nothing personal to share. However, we want to be completely transparent:

  • No Third-Party Sales: We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade any user information to third parties
  • No Marketing Partnerships: We do not share data with marketing companies or advertising networks for targeting purposes
  • No Data Brokers: We do not work with data brokers or participate in data marketplaces
  • Service Providers: We may share anonymous, aggregated statistics with essential service providers (hosting, analytics) who are contractually bound to protect data privacy
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information only when required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights and the safety of our users

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies, prioritizing your privacy:

  • Essential Cookies Only: We use only essential cookies required for basic website functionality
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for advertising, marketing, or behavioral tracking
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not allow third-party tracking cookies or scripts that could compromise your privacy
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage to remember your preferences (theme, language) - this data never leaves your device
  • Cookie Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core streaming functionality
  • Session Management: Any session cookies are automatically deleted when you close your browser

Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect the minimal data we do collect:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is protected using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers and infrastructure follow security best practices and are regularly updated
  • No Data Storage Risk: Since we don't store personal data or video content, there's minimal risk of data breaches affecting personal information
  • Client-Side Processing: All video streaming and processing happens directly in your browser, ensuring your content never passes through our servers
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access our systems and anonymous usage data

Children's Privacy Protection

We are committed to protecting children's privacy online:

  • COPPA Compliance: Our service complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • No Data Collection from Minors: We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age
  • Universal Privacy Protection: Since we don't collect personal information from any users, children are automatically protected
  • Parental Rights: Parents who believe their child has provided information to us can contact us for immediate removal
  • Age-Neutral Service: Our service is designed to be safe for users of all ages without requiring age verification

International Data Transfers

For our international users, we want to clarify our data handling practices:

  • Minimal Cross-Border Data: Since we collect minimal anonymous data, international transfers are extremely limited
  • GDPR Compliance: We comply with European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements
  • Local Processing: Video streaming happens locally in your browser, regardless of your location
  • Data Protection Standards: Any data transfers follow international data protection standards and regulations

Your Privacy Rights

Even though we collect minimal data, you have important rights regarding your privacy:

  • Right to Information: You have the right to know what data we collect (outlined in this policy)
  • Right to Access: You can request information about any data we may have collected
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of any data associated with your usage
  • Right to Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to data processing for legitimate interests
  • Browser Controls: You can control cookies, local storage, and JavaScript through your browser settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements:

  • Notification of Changes: We will post any changes to this privacy policy on this page with an updated revision date
  • Material Changes: For significant changes that affect your privacy rights, we will provide additional notice
  • Regular Review: We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices
  • Continued Use: Your continued use of our service after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated terms
  • Version History: Previous versions of this policy are available upon request

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our privacy practices, we encourage you to contact us:

  • Privacy Inquiries: We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly and transparently
  • Data Requests: For any data-related requests (access, deletion, portability), please contact us through our website
  • Security Issues: If you discover any security vulnerabilities, please report them to us immediately
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days
  • Feedback: We welcome feedback on how we can improve our privacy practices and this policy
